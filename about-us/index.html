ABOUT US

WHO WE ARE

We are a volunteer group with a mission of helping charities through crafting and are founded in Clarksville, Maryland. We were inspired to start this organization because we wanted everyone to be able to volunteer and help others while also having fun and without needing specific skillsets!

OUR MISSION

Crafts for Charity’s mission is to create enjoyable opportunities for those who want to volunteer creatively to help their community. We do this by providing free virtual craft classes that teach the skills needed to donate crafts.

OUR Vision

A world where all have the opportunity to reach their creative potential and provide for the community.

OUR Values

  • We're Caring. We believe in the importance of helping our community.
  • We're a Team. We work together to achieve our goals.
  • We're Creative. We love giving back in unique and innovative ways.
  • We're Diligent. We push ourselves to achieve more each day and deliver the best quality classes and crafts.
  • We're Inclusive. We believe that anyone can learn to help others through art, no matter their skill set!
  • We're Passionate. We’re committed to what we do and want to share our love for creating crafts.
